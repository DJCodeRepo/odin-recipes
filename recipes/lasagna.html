World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is an old family recipe. It's been refined and simplified so anyone can make it.

All of the ingredients combine to make this lasagna my favorite. It's easy and will serve you well. I make it once a month!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
